Visison of FArbucks

With our partners, our coffee and our customers at our core, we live these values:

1. Creating a culture of warmth and belonging, where everyone is welcome.

2.Acting with courage, challenging the status quo and finding new ways to grow our company and each other.

About FArbucks

It was true when the first FArbucks opened in December 2021, and it's just as true today. Back then, the company was a single store in Jakarta. From just a narrow shop front, Farbucks offered some of the finest fresh-roasted whole bean coffees. The name, inspired by Starbuck, evoked the romance of the high seas and the seafaring tradition of the early coffee traders.
