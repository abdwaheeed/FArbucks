Tumbler Day

FArbucks is committed to significantly reducing the waste of plastic and always strives to create a sustainable business. Some of the "green" steps that have been taken include the use of strawless lids to reduce the use of plastic straws, to the "Tumbler Day" program. This program itself is a FArbucks initiative on the 22nd of every month which is inspired by Earth Day which is celebrated on April 22.


Tumbler Day is very special day because not only giving customers a 30-50% discount, every Thursday and the 22nd of every month for their favorite drink if they bring an official FArbuck's tumbler, this program is also one of FArbucks' steps to reduce the use of single-use plastic in stores.

Open Mic Performance

Music fans & players - join our open mic jam on an indoor stage in FArbucks's coffee shoop! Every Sunday from 5pm - 7pm. Players get to perform 2 songs either solo, w/ their mates, or with other musicians from the crowd. We provide a full PA & mics will be sanitized between performance. Other instruments will be run directly thru the PA. You are welcome to bring your own mic & amp. Please sign in when you arrive. BYOB is ok! Please plan to grab a snack from our Cafe - no outside food allowed.

Latte Art Competition

For those applying to compete, a $40 entry fee is required for competitors. You will need to provide five latte art examples with at least three being varying designs. Allowed file types are jpeg, png, and pdf. The maximum file size is 5MB and 1600 pixels by 1600 pixels per image. Please no duplicates, pictures need to be well lit, and we do not accept any etching.
